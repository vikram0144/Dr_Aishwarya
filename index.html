About Me

I completed my BDS in 2019 from the College of Dental Sciences, Davangere, Karnataka, and my MDS in 2023 from MM College of Dental Sciences & Research, Mullana, Haryana, specializing in Conservative Dentistry & Endodontics.

Currently, I am an Attending Consultant at Medanta, Gurugram. As a motivated and confident professional with excellent communication skills, I am always prepared to adapt and excel in any environment. With a strong foundation in healthcare, I have a proven track record of successfully diagnosing and treating various dental conditions. My expertise includes root canals, restorations, esthetics and smile designing, crown preparation, and tooth bleaching.
